## Analytical Method

```math
P(t) = P_1 + t(P_2 - P_1)
```
```math
Q(u) = Q_1 + u(Q_2 - Q_1)
```

For intersection, the two equations must satisfy
```math
\begin{bmatrix}
x_2 - x_1 & -(x_4 - x_3) \\
y_2 - y_1 & -(y_4 - y_3)
\end{bmatrix}

\begin{bmatrix}
t \\ u
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
x_3 - x_1 \\ y_3 - y_1
\end{bmatrix}
```
